Accommodations

Heather Mountain Lodge

The lodge has 22 rooms which makes it an intimate and yet accommodating venue for a wedding. Many of the rooms will be reserved for the wedding party and family, but you are welcome to stay on site. Please indicate on our RSVP page if you are interested in staying for one night or two. Keep in mind that those interested in staying for two nights will get first chance to book for the Saturday night.

Pets: Your furry friends are also welcome to join you if you are staying at the lodge.
