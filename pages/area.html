Accommodations

Heather Mountain Lodge

The lodge has 22 rooms which makes it an intimate and yet accommodating venue for a wedding. Many of the rooms will be reserved for the wedding party and family, but you are welcome to stay on site. Please indicate on our RSVP page if you are interested in staying for one night or two. Keep in mind that those interested in staying for two nights will get first chance to book for the Saturday night.

Pets: Your furry friends are also welcome to join you if you are staying at the lodge.

Other Recommendations

If, by chance, the lodge fills up, Golden is only 30 to 35 minutes away and offers many great options.

Here are a few places we've already checked out ourselves:

  • The Prestige Inn is a very nice Inn where Jason, Alex and Erin stayed on a summer vacation trip to Kelowna.
  • The Aspens at Kicking Horse is where Erin and her bridesmaids stayed when scouting out the town for wedding planning! It was definitely a hit - with 5 girls ready to offer testimonials!
  • Mount 7 Lodges is and ideal place for larger families looking for a getaway

Tourism in the Area

This is your vacation too! We are so excited to have you, and encourage you to take the time and check out the town of Golden and the surrounding Glacier National Park - great hiking and wilderness awaits!
